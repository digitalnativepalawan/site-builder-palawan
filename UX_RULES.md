# UX Enforcement Rules

## Design Principles
- Extreme Simplicity: One primary call-to-action (CTA) per view.
- Mobile-First Integrity: Zero horizontal scrolling; touch-friendly targets.
- Cognitive Load: Hide advanced settings until necessary.

## Implementation Standards
- Linear Progress: Maintain a strict step-by-step flow; no jumping between disconnected states.
- Human-Centric Language: Use industry terms (e.g., "Guest Comforts") instead of technical terms.
- Quality Ceiling: Prioritize a "finished" look out of the box. Output quality is the ultimate metric.
